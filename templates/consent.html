Statement of Informed Consent

You are about to participate in a research study. Your contribution to our research allows us to learn about how news articles are read online, and how social media can be changed to encourage better sharing. Please read the following information carefully before proceeding.

What you will have to do

You will be asked to read snippets from three news articles. Each article is followed by a brief set of questions, including how you might share the article on social media, if you were to do so. In total, this task should take less than 15 minutes.

There are no risks anticipated in taking part in this study and you are free to leave at any time.

To contact the researcher

By accepting this HIT, you confirm that you have read and understood the above and agree to take part in this research. Your participation is voluntary and you are free to leave the experiment at any time by simply closing the web browser.
